About UIC

Dr. Manisha Malhotra
Additional Director UIC CHANDIGARH UNIVERSITY

UIC offers a 2-year regular Master's degree in Computer Applications (MCA) with a unique concept to opt for specialization in 2 years among the emerging areas of modern computing like System Programming, Database Management, Networking, Web-Designing and Artificial Intelligence. The broad objective of the MCA programme at UIC, Best MCA College in Punjab and North India, is to prepare the students for IT industry with Global competence in cutting-edge technologies like Cloud Computing, Android Applications, JAVA, Oracle, .NET, PHP, CISCO and many other which gradually propel them in their career growth. Pursue MCA course from ranked best University Institute of Computing in India for Masters of Computer Applications (MCA).
